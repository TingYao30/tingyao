About me

I am currently a second-year PhD student at Prosodic and Gestural Studies Group (GrEP-G) at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (Barcelona, Spain), under the supervision of Dr. Pilar Prieto. I hold a M.A. in Theoretical and Applied Linguistics at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (Barcelona, Spain).

I am specialized in second language acquisition (SLA). My research focuses on implementing a multimodal narrative intervention in second language (L2) English classroom to promote learning L2 oral and written narrative skills, as well as L2 pronunciation.
